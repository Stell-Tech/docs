Every program has a **pass template** — the design and content that all of its passes inherit: colors, logo, images, text fields, and barcodes. The template editor lets you fine-tune it with a live preview.

![Pass template editor](/images/portal/programs/template-editor.png)

To open the editor, go to your program and click **Edit template** in the **Wallet card template** section.

## How the editor works

The left side shows a live preview of your pass — front and back. The right side holds the controls, organized in tabs.

<Tip>
The fastest way to find the right control is to click the pass itself: click the background to open colors, click an image to open that image's upload, click a field to open its editor.
</Tip>

| Tab | What you edit |
|---|---|
| **Settings** | Organization name (shown on the pass), description, and pass style |
| **Design** | Background color (with **Suggest colors** based on your artwork), visual options, and images — logo, strip, icon, and more, each with its required dimensions |
| **Content** | Logo text, the pass fields (header, primary, secondary, auxiliary, and back fields), and expiration dates |
| **Advanced** | Barcodes, locations (show the pass on the lock screen near your stores), sharing settings, and pass actions |
| **Localization** | Translations of the pass content for each language you support |
| **Semantic** | Structured data the wallet can use, like loyalty or event details |

## Saving changes

1. Make your edits — the preview updates as you go. A colored dot next to the template name warns you about validation issues.
2. Click **Save**. The save dialog summarizes your changes and lists any errors (which block saving) or warnings (which don't).
3. Click **Confirm Save**.

<Warning>
Saving applies your changes to **all existing passes immediately** — every customer's pass updates to the new design, not just passes issued from now on.
</Warning>

Click **Discard** to throw away unsaved changes. If you navigate away with unsaved changes, your browser asks you to confirm first.

## Good to know

- The template belongs to its program — you can't move it to another program from the portal.
- Image uploads have fixed dimensions per slot (shown next to each upload). Using the right size keeps the pass crisp on every device.
- Apple Wallet and Google Wallet render some elements differently; the preview shows the Apple Wallet layout. Check a real pass on both platforms after big changes.
