## Where to start

If your company is new to Stell, work through the [merchant onboarding checklist](/onboarding/checklist) first — it ends with a live program. From there, [how enrollment works](/enrollment/how-enrollment-works) shows what your customers will experience, and the [promotion section](/promote/overview) helps you bring them in.
