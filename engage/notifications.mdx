The **Notifications** page is the audit trail of Engage: every push notification that has been sent — or is about to be — across campaigns, automations, and direct sends.

![Notification history](/images/portal/engage/notifications.png)

## Reading the history

Notifications are grouped under **All**, **Pending**, **Sent**, and **Failed**. Each row shows:

- **Message** — the delivered text, linking to the pass where relevant.
- **Source** — what produced it: a **Campaign** (links to the campaign), an **Automation** (links to the automation), or **Direct** (sent to a single contact from their detail view).
- **Program**, **Status** (Pending, Sent, or Failed), and the sent time.

Click a notification to see its full details.

## Troubleshooting

- **Pending** notifications are queued — scheduled campaigns sit here until their send time.
- **Failed** notifications are kept under the **Failed** tab for troubleshooting. A common cause is the customer removing the pass from their wallet after the message was queued. If the same audience keeps failing, [contact support](/support/contact) with the notification details.
